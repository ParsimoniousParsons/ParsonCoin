Where Does ParsonCoin LLC. Get its Name?

Founded on Sept. 4, 1984, the Parsons were a historical cult in which the godess of Parsimony was worshipped. This cult died when a great fire engulfed their meeting place on Oct. 5, 1894 (See the Parsimonious Fire of '84). The cult was short-lived by all accounts. The main contribution to society that this cult is attributed to is the massive advancements it made in the realms of cryptography and encryption. The modern world of cryptocurrency could not exist without the Parsons of the goddess of Parsimony.

What is the ParsonCoin LLC's Mission?

Named after such a historical institution with ties to some of the greatest advancements in human history, we strive to make innovations like the Parsons did.
